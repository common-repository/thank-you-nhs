Adding a rainbow cursor and banner to your site to spread hope and say thank you to the NHS and key workers.

== Description ==

This plugin allows users to show their support to the NHS and keyworkers by displaying a banner on the website, as well as a rainbow cursor.
If you would like to donate to NHS Charities Together and show your appreciation to all the staff in the NHS and all other keyworkers then please [**visit our JustGiving page**](https://www.justgiving.com/fundraising/sl-plugin-donation) and give anything you can.
Stay at home. Save lives. Protect the NHS.

== Installation ==

= From within WordPress =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'Thank You NHS'
1. Activate from your Plugins page.

= Manually =

1. Upload the `thank-you-nhs` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
